Cyber Awareness Hub

Learn how to recognize cyber threats and protect yourself online. Small precautions can prevent major cyber incidents.

🛑 Phishing Attacks

  • Never share OTPs.
  • Check website URLs carefully.
  • Avoid clicking unknown links.

📱 Fake Applications

  • Download from trusted stores.
  • Check developer details.
  • Read reviews before installing.

🔗 Malicious Links

  • Inspect links before clicking.
  • Avoid shortened URLs from strangers.
  • Verify domains carefully.

💬 Scam Messages

  • Ignore lottery and prize scams.
  • Verify sender identity.
  • Never share banking details.

Top Cyber Safety Tips

✅ Use strong and unique passwords.
✅ Enable two-factor authentication (2FA).
✅ Keep devices and apps updated.
✅ Never share OTPs or passwords.
✅ Verify payment requests before sending money.

What To Do If You Are Scammed

1️⃣ Stop communicating with the scammer.
2️⃣ Change your passwords immediately.
3️⃣ Contact your bank if money is involved.
4️⃣ Report the incident through the portal.
Stay Alert. Stay Secure.
